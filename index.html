The Problem

Education is taught in silos. Nobody draws the lines between subjects.

A student studies the Great Flood in History. The same student studies rivers in Geography. Displacement in English. Nobody ever tells them these are the same event.

01
The Isolation Problem

Every subject teaches in its own lane. History, Geography, Science, English—all studying the same world through separate windows. The connections are real. They are just never made.

02
Missing the Whole Picture

Students can recall facts but cannot transfer knowledge. They pass exams and leave school unable to explain how the world actually works. The connections were never made because nobody was paid to make them.

03
No Data Exists

Schools have subject grades. What they have never had—what has never existed—is a measurement of how well a student connects knowledge across subjects. Until now.

The Solution

One event. Six subjects. Data schools have never had.

A Ripple Module takes a single moment in history—a flood, a moon landing, a nuclear disaster—and teaches it through every subject simultaneously.

History, Geography, English, Science, Maths, Ethics. Each subject reveals something the others cannot. Together, they reveal everything.

At the end of every module, students receive a Ripple Score™—a cross-subject understanding profile that shows not just what they know, but how well they connect what they know.

How It Works

Three steps. Six subjects. One score.

01
Choose your Ripple Module

Select from our library of modules, each built around a single moment that changed history. The Great Mississippi Flood. The Moon Landing. Chernobyl. Each event is a lens through which every subject becomes visible at once.

02
Explore every subject

Work through History, Geography, English, Science, Maths and Ethics—each one revealing a different dimension of the same event. AI responds to every answer individually, pushing thinking deeper, rewarding genuine understanding over surface recall.

03
Earn your Ripple Score™

At the end, receive a Ripple Score across all six subjects: Needs More, Good Thinking, Strong Answer, Ripple, Deep Ripple, Brilliant. The score doesn’t just measure what you know—it measures how you think.
