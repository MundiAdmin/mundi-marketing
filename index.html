The Problem

Education is taught in silos. Nobody draws the lines between subjects.

Isolation

Every subject teaches in its own lane. A student studies the causes of World War One in History. They study trench warfare in English. They study the geography of the Western Front in Geography. Nobody ever tells them these are the same event.

Missing the whole picture

Students can recall facts but cannot transfer knowledge. They pass exams and leave school unable to explain how the world works. The connections were never made because nobody was paid to make them.

No data exists

Schools have subject grades. They have attendance records. They have predicted grades. What they do not have — what has never existed — is a measurement of how well a student connects knowledge across subjects. Until now.

The Solution

One event. Every subject. For the first time — data on how students connect knowledge.

A Ripple Module takes a single moment in history — a flood, a moon landing, a nuclear disaster — and teaches it through every subject simultaneously. History, Geography, English, Science, Maths, Ethics. Each subject reveals something the others cannot. Together, they reveal everything.

At the end of every module, students receive a Ripple Score™ — a cross-subject understanding profile that shows not just what they know, but how well they connect what they know. It is data schools have never had.

How It Works

Three steps. Six subjects. One score.

01
Choose your event

Select from our growing library of Ripple Modules. Each one is built around a single moment in history that changed everything. The Great Mississippi Flood. The Moon Landing. Chernobyl. Each event is a lens through which every subject becomes visible.

02
Explore every subject

Work through History, Geography, English, Science, Maths and Ethics — each one revealing a different dimension of the same event. AI responds to every answer individually, pushing thinking deeper, rewarding genuine understanding.

03
Earn your Ripple Score™

At the end of the module, receive a Ripple Score across all six subjects. Needs More. Good Thinking. Strong Answer. Ripple. Deep Ripple. Brilliant. The score doesn't just measure what you know — it measures how you think.
