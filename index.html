The Problem

Education is taught in silos. Nobody draws the lines between subjects.

A student studies the Great Flood in History. The same student studies rivers in Geography. The same student reads about displacement in English. Nobody ever tells them these are the same event.

01
The Isolation Problem

Every subject teaches in its own lane. History, Geography, Science, English — all studying the same world through separate windows. The connections are real. They're just never made.

02
Missing the Bigger Picture

Students can recall facts but cannot transfer knowledge. They pass exams and leave school unable to explain how the world works. The connections were never made because nobody was paid to make them.

03
No Data Exists

Schools have subject grades. What they have never had — what has never existed — is a measurement of how well a student connects knowledge across subjects. The Ripple Score™ changes that.

The Platform

At the end, every student gets a Ripple Score™ — six subject scores from one event. A cross-curricular understanding profile that has never existed before.

How It Works

Three steps. Six subjects. One score.

01
Choose your Ripple Module

Select from our library of modules, each built around a single moment that changed history. The Great Mississippi Flood. The Moon Landing. Chernobyl. Each event is a lens through which every subject becomes newly visible.

02
Explore every subject

Work through History, Geography, English, Science, Maths and Ethics — each revealing a different dimension of the same event. AI responds to every answer individually, pushing thinking deeper, rewarding genuine understanding over surface recall.

At the end, receive a Ripple Score across all six subjects. Needs More. Good Thinking. Strong Answer. Ripple. Deep Ripple. Brilliant. The score measures not what you know — but how you think. Teachers see every result in real time.
